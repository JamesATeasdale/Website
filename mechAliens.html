Mechanical Aliens Game!

This is a game I developed alongside a great game developer called Frank from Frank's laboratory. He released a tutorial on how to develop his 2D game on FreeCodeCamp's YouTube and supplied us with his assets for free. I learned a lot about JavaScript and how to tie a game to a webpage with HTML and CSS and I am very excited to work on a few of his other projects. While going through this process, I ensured that I understood every step before typing it and felt like I learned a great deal. I'm very proud of what I've accomplished.




    How to play:

  • Reach a score of 100 before time hits 30 seconds
  • W goes up
  • S goes down
  • Space bar shoots
  • Q enters debug mode
  • Good luck!
