Calorie Calculator

Hello, this is my calorie calculator project. One day I decided to walk to town to get a few vaccinations and it took me a whole day. On my way back, I started to wonder how many calories I burn every day, whether I walked or not.

The Rundown

Unfortunately, the embedded spreadsheet does not allow you to input your own details, so if you want to play with it yourself, I encourage you to download it Here.

On the right side, we have a form where you input your information: Weight, Height, Age and Activity.

In the second row, you can see a list called Change(KG). This is a list of how much weight in kg you are expected to lose by following the next two columns.

For example, if you wanted to lose 1KG a week, you would aim to eat the amount of calories on the same column under Daily(Kcal).

The time in the next column (under Weekly(Kcal)) is how many calories you will be eating a week to lose 1KG.

Lastly, we have the first column (apologies for the terrible order). This is a special column that works out how many calories you should eat a day to lose 1KG. This row always equals a 1KG loss, because it was an experiment. And to make things more confusing for you!

That's about it for this small project. It isn't very well organised since it was a personal pet project that wasn't designed for public consumption, but I'm very proud at how functional it is!
I hope you are impressed with this little project. I had a lot of fun making it.
